Research

I am currently a postgraduate student in MUSIC Lab at School of Biomedical Engineering, Health Science Center, Shenzhen University, supervised by Prof. Dong Ni

My research interest is medical image computing. Specifically, I mainly focus on Reinforcement Learning in medical image analysis.
