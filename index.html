Introduction

I am currently a visiting scholar at Harvard Medical School and Boston Children's Hospital, supervised by Dr. Davood Karimi and Prof. Ellen Grant. I received my Ph.D. degree from Shenzhen University in 2025.12, and my supervisor is Prof. Dong Ni.

My research interest is medical image computing. Specifically, I mainly focus on Reinforcement Learning in medical image analysis and robust deep learning model.
